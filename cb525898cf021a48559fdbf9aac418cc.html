Why is money "running away" from us?

Everybody complains about the lack of money. And there are good reasons for this!You see, we are an easygoing people, intuitive and practical. Not so much "on the nose" and "with noses". So if you still don't have a lot of money, then you need to understand this situation from the point of view of financial well-being.It will help you to "make friends" with your own money and get rid of the ever-empty wallet.Here's what you should do to do this::1) Do not be lazy to increase your income This means that you should get rid of "money pit" as soon as possible and in any number.2) Start working on your beliefs about moneyFirst, you should get rid of the pervasive belief that wealth is the lot of the rich.Awareness of this misconception will help you become the richest person in the world.3) Pay yourself every time you spend moneyIf you do not keep track of your expenses, there is an extremely high probability that you will lose your job. So think about it: every month or an amount of money that you receive.4) Make your own personal financial plan (written in the third person)If you have not yet started doing this, then I recommend that you do it now.It is these thoughts that will allow you to gradually form an impressive capital, the income from which will fully provide money for your entire family.5) Look for additional earningsThis is probably the most powerful piece of advice that each of us can give our money. Have a stash, at least a small amount. spare change.And be sure to write it out every time you spend some of it.Because it will be necessary for you to constantly save some part of your income (for a new phone, for example).When you have a "financial cushion" and you have "earned money", you will be able to do many interesting things.And many of them will not be possible, because you will not be in a "rugged" state.6) Make your money work for youHave you ever wondered why your salary is low, and you are so scared every time you get a new salary? It's not your fault.
