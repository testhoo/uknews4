How to stop being afraid to be rich (and become one)

We are all familiar with the saying: "who wants to be rich, has a lot of money, and it's never been easier to be rich."Just want to say that it's not about the fact that you have a lot of money. It's all about how you handle your money:How you treat your money is the key to your success or the lack of success. In this article, we will talk about how to change your mindset and become rich, find financial prosperity and material wealth. well-being.Let's face it today is probably the most interesting time in the history of the world.For the first time in human history, there are serious doubts about the sincerity of the promises of political parties and bankers (about the "financial cushion" and the "safety cushion"). And if you are used to doing nothing, then today is probably the perfect time to act.Yes, it may not be possible to "make friends" with money — but we will definitely be able to change our financial habits and become rich.To do this, it is important to have:1) a set of realistic financial goals.Forget about the fairy tales about the "colossal" money fairy and the "flirty" princeling. You don't need to hold on to past high school memories for high school graduation caps.It is better to set realistic financial goals for yourself.Forgive yourself for lack of success, but it's not for nothing that the expression "it is not for nothing that the bride goes to the market" comes to mind. 2) Start saving money.Today, not only will your pre-pensioner be "on pins", but also every resident of your family will be "on the money".It's not about how much money you make today. Today, you need to start saving money and regularly invest this money in the stock market.It's about how you spend your earned money today.This is where your "money nest" and retirement come in handy.3) Start investing.Today, funds can be started investing (that is, they can be opened to receive interest).Yes, it won't make you a billionaire, but it will help you improve your financial situation and gradually start generating passive income.That's why I always advise my readers to start investing — to increase their money (by regularly investing a small part of their income). for the year).This will allow you will gradually form an impressive capital, the income from which will fully provide you and your entire family. 1) Read my article " Where NOT to invest money?
